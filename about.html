Welcome to Royal Canin Pet Planet

At Royal Canin Pet Planet in Vinob Nagara, Davanagere, we're passionate about providing premium pet accessories that combine style, comfort, and functionality. What started as a small local shop has grown into Shivamogga's favorite destination for designer dog collars, cat collars with bells, and stylish pet belts.

We believe every pet deserves to look and feel their best, which is why we carefully select and design each product in our collection. Our team of pet lovers is dedicated to helping you find the perfect accessories for your furry family members.

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2015, Royal Canin Pet Planet began as a small storefront in KR Puram with just a handful of pet accessories. Our founder, a lifelong animal lover, noticed a lack of stylish yet functional pet products in Shivamogga and set out to change that.

Today, we're proud to serve pet owners across Karnataka with our growing selection of high-quality pet accessories. While we've expanded our product line and moved to a larger location in KR Puram, we've never lost sight of our commitment to personalized service and community values.
